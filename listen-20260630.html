Pick a language, then press Play.
Want the most natural voice? Audio uses the voices on your device (free). The player automatically picks the highest-quality one available, but the premium voices are a one-time free download:
• iPhone/iPad: Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → pick the language → download a voice (e.g. Enhanced / Premium).
• Mac: System Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → System Voice → Manage Voices → download the Enhanced/Premium voice for each language.
• Android: already uses Google's natural voices.
• Desktop: opening this page in Microsoft Edge gives free "Online (Natural)" neural voices.
The status line above shows which voice is playing.
